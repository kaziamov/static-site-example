# Order Notifications

Order Notifications sends operational alerts by Email or Slack when a Shopify
store receives a new order. Merchants choose who gets notified, which orders
qualify, and what details each recipient is allowed to see.

## What it does

- Sends new-order notifications to suppliers, warehouse teams, service
  providers, managers, or internal teams.
- Supports Email and Slack delivery.
- Lets each recipient see only the fields selected by the merchant.
- Keeps delivery history for review and troubleshooting.
</article>

<article class="app-card" markdown="1">
## What it does not do

- It does not modify orders, products, inventory, or customers.
- It is not an analytics or reporting dashboard.
- It does not add storefront tracking, advertising cookies, or customer-facing
  scripts.
</article>
